Korean Beef

 Posted on May 1, 2017  •  Other languages: fr

This korean-inspired dish is a staple in my home. The flavors are very addictive and it is very simple and fast to make. Perfect for a weeknight dinner!

Informations

Portions 2 (big portions)
Difficulty Easy
Cost Cheap
Reviews NA/5
Total Time 20 min
Preparation Time 5 min
Cooking Time 15 min

Categories

#Korean #Beef #Garlic #Ginger #MainDish #Rice #Weeknight

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup of rice
  2. 300 g ground beef
  3. 3 spring onions
  4. 1 garlic clove (minced)
  5. 3 tablespoons of soya sauce
  6. 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  7. 1 teaspoon of minced ginger

Instructions

  1. Start to cook the rice in parallel of the following steps.
  2. Heat a pan with neutral oil on high heat, mince the garlic and roast it in the pan.
  3. When fragrant, add the beef to the pan and press it such as the ground beef crumbles.
  4. When the meat is browned, then add the soya sauce, the ginger and sugar.
  5. Let cook for 5-10 min on low heat until the rice is ready.
  6. In parallel, cut the spring onions in small slices.
  7. When rice is cooked, dispose it in a bowl, serve the beef on top and garnish with the spring onions. Bon app√©tit!

Note: You can also add some vegetables along side the beef such as carrots or cucumbers.
