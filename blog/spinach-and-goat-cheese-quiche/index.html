Spinach and Goat Cheese Quiche

 Posted on June 9, 2018  •  Other languages: fr

Informations

Portions 8 servings tart
Difficulty Easy
Cost Cheap
Total Time 50 min
Preparation Time 10 min
Cooking Time 40 min

 

This is a basic recipe for shortcrust pastry, which can be used for sweet tarts or savory quiche.

Adapted from Marmiton

 

#Quiche #Spinach #Cheese #Vegetarian #GoatCheese

Ingredients

  1. 1 shortcrust pastry ( recipe here)
  2. 500 g of fresh spinach
  3. 100 mL heavy cream
  4. 4 eggs
  5. 1⁄2 goat cheese roll

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven at 180$^{\circ}$C.
  2. Cut half of the goat cheese in 1⁄2 cm thick slices. Cut the rest in small cubes.
  3. Cook the spinach in a pan, until they render all water. Out of the heat, press the spinach to make them render even more water. Too much water in the spinach would soak the quiche and the shortcrust pastry too much.
  4. Place the cooked spinach in a mixing bowl, add the 4 eggs, the cream and the goat cheese cubes.
  5. Pour the egg mix onto the shortcrust pastry.
  6. Add the goat cheese on the quiche in a nice pattern and place into the oven for 40 min (until the shortcrust pastry is golden)
  7. Let cool down a bit and bon appétit!

✎ Tip for quiche and tarts, stamp the dough with a fork before adding the egg mix to avoid rising during baking
